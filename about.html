About AI-CORE

Modern SaaS Landing Page Template

AI-CORE is a premium landing page template designed for SaaS products, AI tools, and tech startups. Built with modern web standards and conversion optimization in mind.

Design Philosophy

Our design combines minimalism with futuristic aesthetics, featuring:

  • Glassmorphism Effects: Frosted glass cards with backdrop blur for a modern, layered look
  • Neon Accents: Subtle cyan glows and borders that create visual hierarchy
  • Smooth Animations: Carefully crafted transitions and progressive loading effects
  • Dark & Light Modes: Full theme support with seamless switching
  • Mobile-First: Responsive design that works beautifully on all devices

Technical Features

Built with cutting-edge web technologies and best practices:

  • Vanilla JavaScript: No framework dependencies, lightweight and fast
  • CSS Custom Properties: Easy theming and customization
  • Accessibility (A11y): ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, screen reader support
  • Performance Optimized: Minimal dependencies, optimized animations
  • SEO Ready: Semantic HTML, meta tags, and structured content
  • Cross-Browser: Tested on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge

Customization

AI-CORE is designed to be easily customizable:

  • CSS Variables: Change colors, spacing, and effects in one place
  • Modular Sections: Add, remove, or reorder sections as needed
  • Content Blocks: Replace demo content with your own text and images
  • Style Guide: Comprehensive documentation of design tokens

Conversion Focused

Every element is designed to drive conversions:

  • Clear call-to-action buttons with visual hierarchy
  • Email capture form in hero section for lead generation
  • Social proof through testimonials and ratings
  • Transparent pricing with feature comparisons
  • FAQ section to address common objections
  • Loading states and feedback for better UX
